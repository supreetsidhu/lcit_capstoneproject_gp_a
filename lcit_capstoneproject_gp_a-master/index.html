FEATURES

Saves Time

Time is Money, right? With Go-Cart-In you save on long queue wait periods and can have yourself extra time for more important things in your life like gas-refill for your vehicle.

Shop Safe

It's imperative that consumers have the ability to checkout using unattended shopping for their health and safety, as well as the health and safety of tireless store workers.

Self Reliant

You need not worry about incorrect billing or missing out on things at the checkout counter. Be your own cashier and do responsible shopping on the go.

Saves Effort

Save your energy in looking out for things in your cart to make sure whether you already added them to your cart or not and keeps a check on your digital shopping list.

GO-CART-IN

Feel free to be a shoppaholic, nobody would judge you.

Do your regular shopping but before you put it in your cart, make sure to scan the item or the barcode using the camera module installed on your shopping cart. Once it's added to your cart, you'll be able to have access to your real-time shopping list and check individual product prices and your total bill on the go.

CHECKOUT

Skip the queue. Scan, pay and go!

When you're done shopping, skip the queue and pay within the app using credit/debit/apple-pay options. If the payment is in cash, use our Assistance feature to request a Cash-checkout.
